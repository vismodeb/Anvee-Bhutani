Anvee Bhutani is an award-winning British American journalist

who’s worked for a range of top global outlets including BBC, The Times, The Telegraph, NBC, Channel 4 and more.

She has reported across four continents and has covered topics ranging from the Moroccan earthquake, US-Mexico border migrant crisis, Palestinian refugees in Lebanon, British universities’ #MeToo movement and more.

Ahead of the 2024 US Presidential election, she will be working on the production team for the leading American political analysis cable TV program, MSNBC The Weekend.

Bhutani can usually be found in NYC, SF, London or Delhi but she works on assignments worldwide speaks Spanish and Hindi alongside a bit of French and Arabic. She is a graduate of the University of Oxford where she served as student body president and Editor-in-Chief of the campus newspaper.
